Privacy Policy

1. Local First Architecture

KAI is designed with a strict "Local First" philosophy. The application runs entirely on your Windows machine using Electron and Python. We do not operate external servers to process your voice commands or store your personal data.

2. Memory Core & Recalls

The Memory Core feature stores your contextual preferences, identity details, and recalls locally on your hard drive. This data is never transmitted to external clouds or third-party servers. You have full control and can delete all memory files directly from the Recalls interface within the app at any time.

3. Microphone Usage

KAI requires microphone access to function as a voice assistant. Audio processing for wake words (such as "hey kai") and voice commands is processed locally on your device. We do not record, store, or transmit your audio streams.

4. System Integration

To execute commands like "Open VS Code" or "Search my files", KAI requires local OS permissions. The application interacts directly with the Windows operating system to perform these tasks securely. No data regarding your file structure or application usage is sent externally.

5. Contact & Support

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or KAI's data practices, please reach out via the contact links provided in the footer below.
